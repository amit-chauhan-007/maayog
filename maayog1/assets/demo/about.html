About

Yoga

Yoga - the most powerful all-encompassing of the various health and fitness regimen a person can follow, which connects an individual from within i.e. inner consciousness to the universal level of consciousness, is an ancient Indian knowledge of body that dates back to over 5000 years. The word Yoga is derived from Sanskrit word which means “Union”. Yoga leads to the union of body and mind. The path of union and the practice of Yoga enables the integration of physical, mental and spiritual well-being.

In the modern era, the expectations have changed a lot and understanding of the concept of Yoga has transformed that can be easily incorporated in our busy lifestyle.

Yoga the way of life

Yoga has evolved a lot and taken different forms to cater the needs of the changing time. Still the principles laid in Yoga Sutra of Patanjali is relevant in todays practice. maaYog offers the traditional yoga practice in the way body requires. Each body and mind is unique and the needs may differ from person to person. We cater the needs of all under one roof and make yoga possible for everyone with varying requirements.

Enable Yoga practice for all and creating wellness in all aspect of life to form a wonderful society.
